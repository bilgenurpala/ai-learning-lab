Project: Blackjack
Write a fully functional Blackjack game.
Rules:

Use a function to deal a random card from the deck
Use a function to calculate the score of a hand — handle Ace as 11 or 1
Use a function to display the cards
Player goes first — can "hit" or "stand"
Dealer reveals hidden card after player stands — must hit under 17
Compare scores and declare the winner
Handle all edge cases:

Blackjack (21 in first 2 cards) ✅
Bust (over 21) ✅
Ace switching from 11 to 1 ✅


Ask if the player wants to play again
